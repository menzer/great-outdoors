Washington Park Arboretum

5.2mi | 3,260ft

Just north of Seattle's Downtown is Green Lake. In such an urban area, Green Lake has been shaped into part of the urban landscape, providing all sorts of recreation activities accessible to everyone. The loop consists of a paved path around the edge of the lake, and provides lanes for both pedestrians and cyclists. Along the loop, there are many opportunities to explore parks, get food and coffee, go swimming, visit the zoo, play sports, and more! Green Lake is a go-to destination for summer time fun.

Getting There

📍 49° 11' 30.5" N, 125° 16' 57.6" W

Follow WA-99 across Aurora bridge, and turn off after passing through Woodland Park.
