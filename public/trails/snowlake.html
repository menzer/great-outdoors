Snow Lake Trail

5.7mi | 1000ft

As you level up in your hiking experience, Snow Lake trail is a nice ease in to climbing steeper hills. Situated right near Snoqualmie Pass, this trail offers rewarding views for inntermediate hikers. Pack yourself a lunch, and enjoy the view overlooking the baisn. There are many sections exposed to the sky, so remember to pack sun screen.

Getting There

📍 49° 11' 30.5" N, 125° 16' 57.6" W

Follow I-90 to Snowqualmie Pass.
