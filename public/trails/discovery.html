Discovery Park Loop

4.4mi | 400ft

On the tip of the Magnolia peninsula is Discovery Park. With forest, meadows, and coasts, Discovery Park offers a taste of the best of the great outdoors without leaving the comfort of Seattle. The expedition to the tip of the peninsula to see the lighthouse is worth it to see the sweeping views across the Puget Sound. The Discovery Park Loop trail is an excelent introduction to what hiking has to offer.
