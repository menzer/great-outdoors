Mt. Margret

11.0mi, 2820ft

A hidden gem next to Mount Saint Hellens, Mt. Margret offers spectacular views overlooking the surrounding lakes. There are defined campsites at Bear Camp, as well as ammenities. The views offer a wide variety of terrain.
