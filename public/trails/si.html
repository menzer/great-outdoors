Mt. Si Trail

8.0mi, 1000ft

Mt. Si is excelent training to level up your hiking game, with secondary options to opt out if you decide it is not for you. You should be comfortable with steep inclines and consider bringing trekking poles if you are newer to hiking. Due to it's vacinity to the city, many decide to make it a training regimend and sprint up and down to build endurance. The top offers a bouldering excercise to the summit.

Getting There

📍 49° 11' 30.5" N, 125° 16' 57.6" W

Take the Trailhead Direct bus.
